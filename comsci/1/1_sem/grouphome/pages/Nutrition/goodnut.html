Why Good Nutrition is Important

Im sure nobody will argue the need for food as humans. However there is the question of what food. Nutrition is simply a process of nourishment, allowing organisms to grow and produce energy. By the simple fact that it is so key to our normal bodily function, you should feel the need to consider what nutrition you are gaining from the foods that you are eating.

But I'm slim

You might believe that being slim and not over eating classifies one as a healthy individual. But this couldn't be further from the truth, your body needs more than just pure calories for ample sustenance. In fact your body needs a mix of energy(calories), minerals and vitamins. You may have heard of the latter before, but didnt quite understand their importance. They are basically components of food that our body needs for growth and repair, I discuss them more in detail along with minerals in this article here

Reap the rewards

Regardless of whether you are an athlete or someone who sits around all day, the benifits of good nutrition are imense. When you provide your body with what it needs, you are rewarded with lower risk of disease, general livelyness, improved cognition and higher life expectancy. With the mentioned you will feel more equip in whatever work or studies you partake in.

By giving time to your health you will save time in your work

Where to start

Its not easy to change your entire diet and drastic change like this almost always fails. You need to take it slow and start eating healthy options for foods you may already be eating. Whether it a placebo or not, upon eating more fruit and vegatables and healthier food in general, you will feel fresh and energized and motivated to continue.
