Where to Find Info on Nutrition

Preface

The internet is great, you have access to a mass of information at you fingertips. However the sources of information are not made equal and the internet is ridden with miss information and bad advice.

I must preface that I am not qualified to give nutritional advice and this article will only comprise information found from cited, reputable sources. The most important take away from this article is to question sources and try to extract evidence based advice on your health and nutrition. Another thing to note is that only health advice does not substitute the need for a certified nutritionist, this is always superior as we our biased in what information we take away from websites.

Who to Trust

How to determine if an article/source is trusted? This is a really important question when looking for advice about health. I personally follow a simple and intuitive list of questions you can ask yourself:

  • Is my judgment of the information presented swayed by it giving an answer I had hoped for?
    • Acknowledge this and ensure even more careful thought on the questions in this list
  • Is the articles website trusted to provide empirical scientific information?
    • Does it come from a well known scientific sector of a university?
    • Does it reference information from the aformentioned?( if so your better off going to the source )
  • Who wrote the article?
    • Is there any bias?
    • Does it look like much thought was given to the article?
  • Do other more reputable sources disregard this information?

Apart from the above, you could also more simply only consider advice or information from trusted sites that I personally use, of course you should make the decision on whether my choice of sites are reasonable. Nonetheless the following will comprise such sites, along with what information they are good for providing.

World Health Organization

The World Health Organization has a tonne of information on various topics involving nutrition as well as health in general. They also reference studies involving such topics to provide an empiracal viewpoint. If I were to choose one site that I could trust information from it would be them.
